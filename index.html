How to Play

1. Assign Roles

Randomly assign the six mission control roles to your team. Each role has a strength and a blind spot, so every voice should be used with awareness.

2. Debate Under Pressure

Work through three stages of the mission. Every crisis card offers three imperfect options. Decide before the countdown runs out or the contingency plan is triggered.

3. Track Trade-Offs

Each decision changes mission success, morale, time, and risk. Fast action can save time but increase risk, while caution protects the team at a cost.

4. Debrief the Dynamics

Use the final OB debrief to discuss communication quality, coordination patterns, and whether the team balanced execution with reflection.
